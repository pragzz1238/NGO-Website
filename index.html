About Us

Our core organization, promotes smart giving and recommends a curated list of highly effective charities proven to dramatically improve life for those living in extreme poverty. We make “smart giving simple” by curating a group of nonprofits that save or improve the most lives. We aim to create a world where everyone has an opportunity to build a better life and where there’s no suffering or death due to extreme poverty.
